The Phoenix Theater Petaluma



Rocky Horror has long been the occisional wild midnight event to hit Petaluma, Starting in the Late 1980"s Indecent Exposure from Berkeley performed special shows at the Phoenix during October, into the 1990's Absolute Pleasure made its home in Petaluma at the Phoenix for Quarterly shows. Barely Legal carries on the tradition of one of the only midnight shows to roll through town. You bet we bring a roudy Cast and Audeince to the Historic Theater that has served the community as a theater, teen center, Skate park, and expressive Punk Rock space for over a century!! We do not have a regular scheduled show at this theater so check our website, or facebook for current showtimes.
