About 3Below


3Below is downtown San Jose's social spot for stage, screen, music, comedy, family fun and events.
We are a small but mighty group of producers, designers, actors, and lovers of the creative and performing arts dedicated to providing quality, accessible, diverse, and entirely enjoyable entertainment events and experiences for audiences of all ages.

3Below is more than just another theater complex it's a gathering place for the community.
Under one roof you'll find three theaters, two production companies, and one singular mission of creating memorable experiences for you and your friends and family. Our three theaters and lounge feature programming six days a week.
